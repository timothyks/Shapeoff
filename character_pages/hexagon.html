Hexagon

Hexagon is a teenage girl that loved destruction as a child, mainly because her parents were the leaders of the most dangerous Mafia in Shape-Land. She wasn’t taken by her parents to most work because they were too busy too, but when they did, she loved it. Since she got taken to school, she has had little contact with her parents, but gradually got more dangerous along her Elementary, Middle, and Most of High School years.

  • Hexagon loved terrorist events like 9/11, but hates abuse.
  • Hexagon is plotting to kill Host to get the prize to herself and give to her parents so they can be the most powerful individuals in Shape-Land.
  • She doesn’t have a lot of friends at school, because she hates people who want to stop evil. (Which are most people.)
