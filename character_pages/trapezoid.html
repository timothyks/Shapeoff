Trapezoid

Trapezoid is a mute young boy who has very quiet parents. But, they are all very intelligent. Trapezoid has learned to do everything shapes with arms can do with only his legs, so he is mostly seen with shapes with arms during school, but, he doesn’t actually know how to speak.

  • Trapezoid has an IQ of 160 and is as smart as a Scientist.
  • Trapezoid’s favorite thing to do is to explore.
  • Trapezoid is the most intelligent shape in the Shape-Land, even smarter than his parents at a young age
  • Trapezoid hates Takis
