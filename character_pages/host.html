Host

Host is a potato-like circle that is very stupid but very powerful, not knowing his true power. He is from a distant planet and to stay on that planet, you had to pass a test when you were 3 years old. Due to his stupidity, he failed the test and got exiled to the Shape-Lands.

  • Host’s first show to watch was Total Drama and that’s why he wanted to run a show so badly.
  • He is attracted to Flabbergasted Peaks because they are very similar in greatness, but he’s too stupid to understand that.
  • He is the only one of his kind to fail the test
  • He hates being interrupted
  • He has a name but can’t remember it because of his stupidity
