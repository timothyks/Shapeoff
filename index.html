Welcome to the Shapeoff Encyclopedia!

Shapeoff has become more than just a goofy object show with minimal effort put into it. Prior to its cancellation shortly after the release of "Shapeoff 7: Hospital Heist," which is currently privated on YouTube due to personal reasons, the directors have been working for hours to expand and flesh out the lore of the Shapeoff world.

So, with that said, I've created an "encyclopedia" for the entirety of Shapeoff lore. There may be some oversights, as all of the lore was created in a single Google Docs document, but I ensure that the lore is still worth checking out.

Lore made by @timks and @ColtonTheBOZO on YouTube
